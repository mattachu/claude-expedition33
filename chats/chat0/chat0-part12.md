Breaker does exactly what it says: increases the Break contribution of all her attacks.

That turns every Maelle action into Break pressure, not just one skill.

**4. Turn frequency (Agility)**

Break is ultimately about how many times you apply pressure before the enemy recovers.

Maelle's high Agility means more actions per fight and more total Break applied.

This is why slowing her slightly was fine, but gutting Agility would not be.

The honest answer is: she has several skills that are "good enough" — none that are uniquely dominant for Break on their own.

That role is closer to Lune (with Crystal Crush) or later Verso setups.

Maelle is the exploiter, not the initiator.
