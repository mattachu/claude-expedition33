1️⃣ Design principles for low-frustration parry learning
These are the constraints that matter more than any specific enemy.

Constraint A: Damage must not end the fight
If your damage is high enough to skip phases, you don’t get repetitions.

Constraint B: Mistakes must be survivable
Learning parry timing requires:

Constraint C: Repetition beats variety
Parry skill is pattern recognition, not reflex.

Therefore:
You want:

few moves

repeated often

with clear telegraphs

Constraint D: Parry should be optional, not required
If the fight requires perfect parries, frustration spikes.

Therefore:
Parry should be a bonus, not a survival gate.
