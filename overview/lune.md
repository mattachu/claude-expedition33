## Mechanics

Lune's central system is **stain generation and consumption**. Free-aim shots (1 AP each) are her primary stain engine: each shot removes shields, deals damage, applies Burn (Burning Shots Lumina), applies Mark (Marking Shots Lumina), and generates one random stain (Trebuchim Level 4). Typical turn: 2–3 free-aim shots to fill stains, then a skill that consumes them.

**Stain elements:** Fire, Ice, Lightning, Earth, Light. Stains are consumed by skills for bonus effects (increased damage, AP cost reduction, Regen, etc.).

**Key stain interactions:**
- Mayhem: consumes ALL stains for damage and Break. Requires 4 stains for Break. Trebuchim free-aim (up to 3 stains) + Wildfire (generates Fire + Light = 2 stains) makes 4-stain Break reliable.
- Healing Light: consumes 2 Earth stains to cost 0 AP.
- Rebirth: consumes 3 Lightning stains to cost 0 AP.
- Trebuchim Level 10: +1 AP per consumption **event** (not per stain — Mayhem consuming 4 stains = +1 AP total, not +4).
- Trebuchim Level 20: base attack generates 2 random stains (not yet unlocked at end of Chat 1).

**Elemental Genesis build (deferred):** Requires exactly 1 of each element stain. Trebuchim generates random stains and is incompatible. Requires Kralim weapon and full rebuild — see Build Options.
