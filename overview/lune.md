## Role in Party

Support/healer/stain DPS. Free-aim is central to her playstyle: each shot (1 AP) removes shields, deals damage (bonus at weak spots), applies Burn (Burning Shots Lumina), applies Mark (Marking Shots Lumina), and generates one random stain (Trebuchim Level 4). Typical turn: 2–3 free-aim shots to fill stains, then skills. Uses Mayhem as primary stain-consuming damage/break skill. Crystal Crush is primary Break damage source. Provides healing via Healing Light (single-target, removes status including Curse), Revitalization (AoE heal), Rebirth (revive). Tree of Life (Gradient Attack) provides party-wide status removal.

Relationship quest (Sirene's Dress) completed — Lune at Relationship Level 7.

## Errors to Avoid

- **Colim recommendation (primary):** Recommended over Trebuchim based on Light stain wildcards. Wrong for playstyle. Root cause: didn't simulate turns, didn't verify AP economy. AP economy is identical (+1 per consumption event) for both weapons. Free-aim stain generation (Trebuchim) gives on-demand control; Colim's turn-start generation does not. 14% attack loss not justified.
- **Trebuchim AP economy misstatement:** Initially implied +1 AP per individual stain consumed. Correct: +1 AP per consumption event. Mayhem consuming 4 stains = +1 AP total.
- **Colim AP economy misstatement:** Same error. Colim Level 10 gives +1 AP when Light stains consumed — also per consumption event.
- **Revitalization removes status effects:** Said it does. Correct: heals only, no status removal. Only Healing Light and Tree of Life remove status.
- **Pelerin Heal removes status effects:** Said it does. Correct: Regen only. (Monoco's skill, but noted here as Lune's healing role informed by this correction.)
- **Tree of Life cost:** Said 1 Gradient. Correct: 2 Gradient charges.
- **"Energising Cleanse" initially confabulated:** Said it didn't exist. Correct: it exists. Effect: Dispels first negative status received + grants 2 AP. Once per battle.
- **Elemental Genesis + Trebuchim compatibility:** Incompatible. Trebuchim generates random stains; Elemental Genesis requires exactly 1 of each element. Recommending Genesis without noting this incompatibility was an error.
- **Mayhem break condition:** Needs 4 stains consumed to Break. With only 2–3 stains, Mayhem deals damage but cannot Break.
- **Choralim as primary Elemental Trick weapon (Part 4):** Described Choralim as the key weapon enabling 100% crit for Elemental Trick loop. Wrong — Choralim gives 100% crit ONLY when 4 stains are already active (circular dependency). Cannot be stain-generation weapon. Base 100% crit must come from Luck stat + crit Pictos independently.
