# Maelle — Clair Obscur: Expedition 33

*Last updated: [2026-03-08 21:12]*

---

## Role in Party

Primary DPS. First in turn order. Excels at single-target burst and trash clearing. Used in both the Act 2–3 main team (Maelle/Lune/Monoco → Maelle/Lune/Sciel) and the planned endgame team (Maelle/Sciel/Verso). Key synergies: Virtuose Stance (entered via Gustave's Homage or Swift Stride); Burn setup enables Swift Stride entry and Breaking Burn multiplier; Mark enables Rewarding Mark AP return. Fencer's Flurry clears trash in one hit if no/few shields. Gustave's Homage → Sword Ballet is primary burst sequence. Stendhal is Virtuose nuke.

## Current Build

### Attributes

End of Chat 1 (Level 63): Agility 99, Defence 76, Luck 7, Vitality 7, Might 0.

Progression: Level 46 had Agility 70, Defence 54; Level ~52 (pre-Paintress) had Agility 82, Defence 63. Continued reallocation toward Agility primary through Act 3 levelling. Agility primary because it scales Speed, Attack (weapon scaling), and Defence stat simultaneously. Defence retained over zero because Defence contributes to Critical Rate (~0.18% per point, vs Luck ~0.36% per point — Luck is 2× more efficient but Maelle doesn't use Luck otherwise, so Defence secondary serves dual purpose). Vitality and Luck near floor (7 each) — low priority because weapon doesn't scale off them.

## Errors to Avoid

- **Fueling Break effect:** Early advice said it gives AP when dealing Break damage. Correct: breaking an enemy doubles Burn stacks on that enemy. Removed from loadout not because effect is wrong but because Maelle uses Burn for enablers (Swift Stride, Breaking Burn) not DoT; the doubling adds limited value.
- **Healing Parry effect:** Stated as 10% heal on parry. Correct: 3%.
- **Last Stand Lumina triggers:** Stated as HP-threshold triggers (below 25% HP). Correct: solo-fighting bonuses only. Irrelevant in party fights.
- **Percée AP cost:** In Virtuose, Percée costs 2 AP — but after use, Maelle leaves Virtuose. Cannot be spammed at 2 AP. One use per Virtuose window.
- **Gustave's Homage stance:** Switches TO Virtuose (not stays in Virtuose). Confirmed by Matt in Part 3.
- **Breaking Rules functionality:** Does not deal Break damage. Only destroys shields (+1 AP per shield, plays second turn if target Defenceless). Correctly replaced by Gustave's Homage.
- **LP budget:** Must not exceed 112 LP. Earlier calculation reached 118 LP (6 over); caught in failure mode check. Always recount.
- **Drop Fencer's Flurry:** In Part 5, Claude said to drop Fencer's Flurry because "you have better AoE options now." Wrong — Fencer's Flurry is Maelle's best AoE for trash clearing. No better AoE option was available. Retract this recommendation.
