## Mechanics

Monoco's central system is the **Bestial Wheel** — a 9-position ring of masks that rotates as skills are used. Each skill advances the wheel by a set number of positions (+2 to +6). The active mask determines bonus effects for that turn (e.g. +1 AP on Caster Mask for Potier Energy, free second turn on Agile Mask for Abbest Wind, increased damage on Balanced Mask for Chalier Combo).

**Wheel layout (9 positions):** Balanced → Balanced → Heavy → Heavy → Almighty → Caster → Caster → Agile → Agile

**Nusaro starts in Agile Mask.** Turn 1 Abbest Wind is therefore free (0 AP on Agile) and advances the wheel by +2.

**Wheel rotation per skill:**

| Skill                 | Wheel | Mask active for bonus                        |
|-----------------------|-------|----------------------------------------------|
| Abbest Wind           | +2    | Agile — costs 0 AP, plays second turn        |
| Stalact Punches       | +4    | Heavy                                        |
| Potier Energy         | +6    | Caster — +1 AP to all allies                 |
| Pelerin Heal          | +3    | Caster — 40% HP heal in addition to Regen    |
| Chalier Combo         | +3    | Balanced — increased damage                  |
| Moissonneuse Vendange | +3    | Balanced                                     |

**⚠️ The ★ symbol on a skill icon means "can Break enemies" — NOT extra wheel movement.**

**Bénisseur Mortar** jumps to Almighty Mask ONLY when the target is Marked. Without reliable Mark, it does not jump — making it a weak standard skill without the current team's Mark frequency.

## Errors to Avoid

- **Nusaro as endgame weapon:** Said Nusaro Level 20 is the endgame weapon. Correct: endgame weapon is Joyaro (Level 28, Flying Manor Lampmaster, post-game).
- **Abbest Wind removal:** Recommended removing it in Part 7 for Évêque Spear and Chalier Combo. Wrong: essential for wheel cycling and free second turn. Matt corrected this.
- **Auto Shell = Base Shield:** Distinct mechanics. Base Shield = 1 damage-absorbing shield buffer per turn; Shell = 20% damage reduction for 3 turns. Monoco has Base Shield via core Lumina; Auto Shell is survivability overkill given his stats.
- **Painted Power as Pictos:** Recommended as a Pictos for Monoco. Wrong: it is a 5 LP Lumina available to all characters — wasting a Pictos slot is inefficient.
- **Healing Counter LP cost:** Listed as 1 LP. Correct: 10 LP.
- **Energising Break frequency overestimated:** Triggers only on Break (1–2 per boss fight). Replaced by Energising Heal.
- **Initial stat advice (0 Defence):** Recommended zeroing Defence without knowing it contributes to crit rate. Testing showed 0 Defence → Crit drops from 23% to 5%.
- **Pelerin Heal status removal:** Said it removes status effects. Correct: Regen only (and 40% heal on Caster Mask).
- **Bestial Wheel ★ symbol:** Mistaken for bonus wheel movement. Correct: ★ = can Break enemies. Unrelated to wheel movement.
- **Confirmation bias on player skill suggestions:** Accepted Évêque Spear and Chalier Combo without independent research in Part 7.
- **Bénisseur Mortar Almighty jump:** Jumps to Almighty ONLY if target is Marked. Not a reliable Almighty access tool without Mark frequency from Lune/Maelle.
