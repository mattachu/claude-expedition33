## Role in Party

AP battery/support/breaker in levelling team (Verso/Monoco/Sciel, Act 3). Reserve in endgame (Maelle/Sciel/Verso). Key function: Potier Energy gives 1–3 AP to all allies (+1 on Caster Mask = 2–4 AP total). Turn order in levelling team: Sciel ~1812 → Verso ~1436 → Monoco ~1163 (Monoco goes last in levelling team, refilling AP after others have spent). Provides healing via Pelerin Heal. Break damage via Stalact Punches (high Break, fills bar) — enabling Verso's Perfect Break to trigger the Break and jump to S Rank. Abbest Wind provides flexible wheel movement + free second turn on Agile Mask. Chalier Combo for high damage on Balanced Mask.

## Errors to Avoid

- **Nusaro as endgame weapon:** Stated Nusaro Level 20 is the endgame weapon. Correct: endgame weapon is Joyaro (Level 28 drop, Flying Manor Lampmaster, post-game). Nusaro → Level 20 is mid-game only.
- **Abbest Wind removal:** In Part 7, recommended removing Abbest Wind in favour of Évêque Spear and Chalier Combo. Wrong: Abbest Wind is essential for mask cycling and free second turn on Agile. Matt corrected this. Cannot be removed.
- **Auto Shell = Base Shield:** Confused Shell and Shield across multiple responses (Part 8). They are distinct: Base Shield = 1 damage-absorbing shield buffer per turn; Shell = 20% damage reduction for 3 turns. They stack but serve different purposes. Monoco has Base Shield via Lumina core; Auto Shell adds Shell, which is overkill given his stats.
- **Painted Power as Pictos:** Recommended Painted Power as a Pictos for Monoco (citing it removes the 9,999 damage cap). Correct: Painted Power (5 LP Lumina) is available to ALL characters — wasting a Pictos slot is inefficient.
- **Healing Counter LP cost:** Listed as 1 LP in a loadout. Correct: 10 LP. Removed once correct cost established.
- **Energising Break frequency overestimated:** Included at 3 LP in core suite. Triggers only on Break (1–2 times per boss fight). Replaced by Energising Heal (triggers more frequently).
- **Initial stat advice (0 Defence):** Recommended removing all Defence before knowing Defence contributes to Critical Rate. Testing showed: 0 Defence → Crit drops from 23% to 5%. Always test stat interactions before recommending 0 allocation.
- **Pelerin Heal status removal:** Claimed it removes status effects. Correct: Regen only (and 40% heal on Caster Mask). Confirmed in Part 3.
- **Bestial Wheel ★ symbol:** Initially thought "+" or "★" symbol next to wheel value indicated bonus wheel movement. Correct: ★ on skill icon = can Break enemies. Unrelated to wheel movement.
- **Confirmation bias on player skill suggestions:** Accepted Matt's Évêque Spear and Chalier Combo suggestions in Part 7 without independent research. Always independently verify before confirming.
- **Troubadour Trumpet effect:** Troubadour applies a random buff to 1–3 allies (2nd random buff on Caster Mask). Not a fixed utility.
- **Bénisseur Mortar Almighty jump:** Jumps to Almighty ONLY if target is Marked. Without active Mark on target, it does not jump. Not a reliable Almighty access tool without the current team's Mark frequency.
- **Vale boss lockout:** Matt asked whether he needed to complete the Vales before fighting the Axon in Visages. Claude said he could come back later and would not miss anything. WRONG — once the Axon is defeated, the Vale bosses (Jovial Moissonneuse, Sorrowful Chapelier, Seething Boucheclier) are permanently locked out via the riddle mechanic. Matt lost access to these bosses (though Moissonneuse Vendange was obtained via an alternate source and Chromatic Ramasseur was also completed). Always warn: defeat Vale bosses BEFORE the Axon.
