# Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 — Historical Error Log

Active failure mode categories extracted from this log are maintained in Section 1 of the overview file. Fetch this file only when reviewing specific past errors.

-----

1. **Vale bosses missable (ChatGPT error, not Claude):** ChatGPT said the Vale bosses could be accessed after defeating the Axon, and that nothing in Visages was permanently missable. This was wrong. Three Vale bosses permanently lost: Jovial Moissonneuse, Sorrowful Chapelier, Seething Boucheclier. Matt had explicitly asked whether to explore before fighting the boss; ChatGPT said he could come back later. Moissonneuse Vendange was obtained separately by defeating a Moissonneuse in Visages — unrelated to the Vale bosses. Retained here as a real consequence affecting this playthrough, and as a category to guard against — but not a Claude error.
2. **Colim recommendation:** Recommended over Trebuchim based on Light stain wildcards and community meta. Wrong for Matt's playstyle. Root error: didn't simulate turns, didn't check playstyle assumptions.
3. **Monoco Defence stat advice:** Recommended 0 Defence without knowing Defence contributes to crit rate. Testing showed 0 Defence dropped crit from 23% to 5%.
4. **Fueling Break effect:** Said it gives AP on Break damage. Correct: doubles Burn stacks when enemy is broken.
5. **"Energising Cleanse" invented with wrong effects:** Claude described an "Energising Cleanse" Lumina with incorrect effects. A real Energising Cleanse does exist but its actual effects are different.
6. **Last Stand Lumina effects:** Described as HP-threshold triggers. Correct: solo-fighting bonuses only.
7. **Perfect Recovery:** Described as party heal. Correct: heals Verso only.
8. **Tree of Life gradient cost:** Said 1 Gradient. Correct: 2 Gradient charges.
9. **Revitalization:** Said it removes status effects. Correct: heals only.
10. **Cleansing Tint Lumina:** Said it removes Curse via skills. Correct: only affects Healing Tint consumable items.
11. **Healing Parry effect:** Said 10% heal on parry. Correct: 3%.
12. **Maelle LP budget overrun:** Calculated 118 LP against a 112 LP budget.
13. **Trebuchim AP economy:** Implied +1 AP per individual stain consumed. Correct: +1 AP per consumption event.
14. **Charnon scaling:** Said Defence + Agility. Correct: Defence + Luck.
15. **Charnon/Litheson quote conflation:** Attributed "god tier" and "changes how you play her" quotes to wrong weapons.
16. **Ramasson + Energising Heal synergy:** Claimed passive heal triggers Energising Heal. Correct: weapon passive heals explicitly cannot trigger on-heal Lumina effects.
17. **Quick Strike rank jump:** Said it jumps to Rank B. Correct: requires Glaceso Level 4 ability. Without Glaceso, Quick Strike gives modest Perfection gain.
18. **Phantom Stars for solo fight:** Recommended for Verso's Golgra solo. AoE is useless in 1v1.
19. **Second Chance Pictos tracking:** Recommended for multiple characters simultaneously. Only one character can hold the Pictos at a time; also only works once per battle.
20. **Paradigm Shift rank jump:** Said it jumps Verso to S Rank. Correct: generates 1–3 AP, with bonus at C Rank.
21. **Lumina skill tree claim:** Said Lumina are unlocked via a skill tree. Correct: unlocked by equipping the corresponding Pictos and winning battles.
22. **Verso's endgame skills assumed available:** Light Holder and End Bringer recommended across multiple summaries. Verso did not have either at the time.
23. **Renoir described as mid-game boss:** Initially placed in Phase 2 of the progression plan. Correct: Renoir is the final boss.
24. **Nusaro as Monoco's endgame weapon:** Said Nusaro should be upgraded to 20 as his endgame weapon. Correct endgame weapon is Joyaro (drops at level 25 from Ultimate Sakapatate in Endless Night Sanctuary, and from Flying Manor Lampmaster; also purchasable from a River of Life merchant).
25. **Chevalam available "right now":** Claimed Chevalam accessible at current level (~56). Recommended level for Chromatic Gold Chevaliere is 70–75+.
26. **Serpenphare placed in Phase 2:** Initially said Level 60–70 was appropriate. Correct recommended level is 70–80+.
27. **Rank bonuses claimed to work at rank and above:** Confirmed by community sources and in-game behaviour: bonuses apply ONLY at the exact rank stated. Passing through a rank means the bonus is active only briefly in that window.
28. **Overload AP usage:** Implied 9 AP could be spent on multiple skills in the same turn. Correct: AP from Overload is available the following turn.
29. **"Plentiful Harvest synergy (6)" as a Lumina:** Listed as a Lumina in a loadout. It is not a Lumina — it was a mislabelled description.
30. **Healing Counter cost:** Listed as 1 LP. Correct: 10 LP.
31. **Shell and Shield confused:** Auto Shell gives 20% damage reduction for 3 turns (Shell). Base Shield gives 1 absorb-type defensive buffer per turn (Shield). Described as interchangeable at one point.
32. **Confirmation bias on Monoco skills:** Accepted Évêque Spear and Chalier Combo immediately when Matt suggested them, without independent research. Both were later revised.
33. **Compaction continuity overclaim:** After a compaction event mid-chat, Claude described the compaction summary as fully preserving all context. Some nuance is lost in compaction; this was slightly misleading.
34. **Flying Manor incorrectly stated as required:** Claimed Flying Manor was a mandatory story chapter that must be completed before Renoir. Correct: it is optional. The only mandatory pre-Renoir story content is The Reacher. The final boss is fought inside Lumière at the end of that dungeon — not at the Monument.
35. **Final boss location:** Said the Act 3 final fight was at the Monument. Correct: the final boss is fought inside Lumière at the end of that dungeon. The Monument fight was the Act 2 climax.
36. **Joyaro drop location:** Said Joyaro drops exclusively from Flying Manor Lampmaster post-game. Correct: also drops from Ultimate Sakapatate in Endless Night Sanctuary (level 25), and is purchasable from a River of Life merchant.
37. **Teamwork Lumina effect:** Said Teamwork gives AP when an ally uses a skill. Correct: passive damage bonus when the whole team is alive.
38. **Stendhal AP cost:** Said 4 AP. Correct: 8 AP. Also applies Defenceless to Maelle herself on use. Was nerfed 40% in Patch 1.2.3 — no longer one-shots Alicia.
39. **Gaulteram described as Act 3 best option:** Gaulteram is consensus Act 2 weapon. Community S-tier Act 3 weapons are Chevalam, Contorso, Corpeso, Simoso.
40. **No in-battle party rotation (ChatGPT error):** ChatGPT claimed party members can be swapped mid-battle. Correct: there is no in-battle rotation mechanic — party composition is fixed for the duration of a fight. Noted as a ChatGPT error but worth recording given Claude's thin training data on game mechanics makes this a plausible confabulation.
